# Net Long Position

## What it is:

The Net Long Position Aggregated indicator uses Open Interest to offer insights into traders' positions in relation to Bitcoin. This indicator, visualized using a candlestick chart, highlights the predominance of long positions in an asset over time.

The Net Long Position estimates the value of the sum of the number of long contracts. When this indicator shows positive values, it indicates a preponderance of long positions over short ones.


Values below zero suggest a greater presence of short positions.

## How to use:

The Net Long Position serves as an indicator of market sentiment. Positive values indicate an optimistic bias, reflecting a more significant presence of optimistic traders (longs). As this indicator grows, it can suggest an increase in bullish sentiment, indicating potential upward movements in the asset's price.

**Negative values**

When the indicator is below zero, this points to a pessimistic bias. A decrease in negative values can indicate an intensification of pessimistic sentiment, signaling potential downward movements in the asset's price.

**Positive values**

When above zero, this characterizes an optimistic bias. An increase in the metric represents an intensification of optimistic sentiment towards the asset.
